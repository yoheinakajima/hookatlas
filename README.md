# Hook Atlas

A playable atlas of **120 game mechanics** ("hooks") translated across games, consumer
apps, SaaS, brand/marketing, religion/community — and high-control anti-patterns —
so founders can design ethical growth loops, onboarding paths and community rituals.

**Open [`index.html`](index.html)** — it's a single self-contained file with no external
scripts, fonts, tracking or network calls.

## What's inside

- **Mechanic Library** — search, family and risk-posture filters, and a detail view for
  each mechanic with founder moves, good/coercive framings, neuro/psych/society lenses
  and citations. On mobile the detail opens as a bottom sheet.
- **Concept Map** — a scatter map of all 120 mechanics arranged by agency vs.
  social/identity intensity, with family spotlighting and nearest-neighbor lookup.
- **Cross-Domain Translation Matrix** — every family translated into games, consumer
  apps, SaaS, brands, communities, plus the high-control red flag to avoid.
- **Founder Opportunity Canvas** — pick a mechanic × domain × posture and generate a
  practical experiment prompt (with metric stack and ethics guardrail) you can copy
  straight into your notes or an AI chat.
- **Ethics guardrails & Citation Library** — 37 cited sources; cult dynamics and dark
  patterns are treated strictly as anti-patterns.

Extras: deep-link any mechanic (`index.html#m=ritual-0`), press `/` to search,
🎲 "Surprise me" jumps to a random mechanic.
