Ethics guardrails

This atlas treats high-control groups, cult dynamics and dark patterns as anti-patterns. The usable founder move is not "copy cults"; it is "understand why belonging, ritual, status and narrative are powerful, then preserve autonomy, informed consent, reversibility and truthful value exchange."

Autonomy

Users can understand the choice, say no, leave, pause, downgrade and recover without humiliation or hidden penalty.

Truthfulness

Scarcity, odds, prices, terms, social proof and testimonials are accurate, current and not simulated.

Proportionality

The mechanic is appropriate for the stakes. Surprise is fine for cosmetic delight; it is not fine for money, privacy or health-critical decisions.

Dignity

The system never requires isolation, confession leverage, leader worship, harassment, coercive recruiting or surrender of independent judgment.
